## Step 1: Set App Pricing

### 1.1 Configure Pricing in App Store Connect

1. **Go to App Store Connect**
   - Visit: https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
   - Sign in with your Apple ID

2. **Navigate to Pricing**
   - My Apps ‚Üí Khandoba ‚Üí Pricing and Availability
   - Click "Edit" next to Price Schedule

3. **Select Price Tier**
   - Choose your price tier (e.g., Tier 8 = $9.99)
   - **Recommended**: Tier 8 ($9.99) or Tier 12 ($14.99)

4. **Set Availability**
   - Select countries (default: All countries)
   - Click "Save"

## Step 6: Build & Upload

### 6.1 Pre-Build Checklist

**In Xcode:**
- [ ] Select "Generic iOS Device" (not simulator)
- [ ] Version: 1.0.1
- [ ] Build: 29 (or next number)
- [ ] Signing: Automatic (Team: Q5Y8754WU4)
- [ ] Configuration: Release

### 6.2 Create Archive

1. **Product ‚Üí Archive**
2. **Wait for archive** (5-10 minutes)
3. **Validate App** in Organizer
4. **Distribute App** ‚Üí App Store Connect ‚Üí Upload

### 6.3 Upload Process

**Using Transporter (Recommended):**
1. Open Transporter app
2. Drag IPA file or click "+"
3. Click "Deliver"
4. Wait for upload (10-20 minutes)

**Using Xcode:**
1. In Organizer, select archive
2. Click "Distribute App"
3. Select "App Store Connect"
4. Click "Upload"
5. Follow prompts
