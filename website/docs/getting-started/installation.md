# Installation & Setup

## System Requirements

### Minimum Requirements
- **iOS**: 17.0 or later
- **Device**: iPhone or iPad
- **Storage**: 100 MB free space (more for documents)
- **Internet**: Required for initial setup and cloud sync

### Recommended
- **iOS**: Latest version
- **Device**: iPhone 12 or later, iPad (any model)
- **Storage**: 1 GB+ free space
- **Biometric**: Face ID or Touch ID enabled

---

## Download from App Store

### Step 1: Open App Store
1. Tap the **App Store** icon on your device
2. Tap the **Search** tab at the bottom
3. Search for **"Khandoba Secure Docs"**

### Step 2: Install
1. Tap **"Get"** or the price button
2. Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
3. Wait for download to complete

### Step 3: Open App
1. Tap the **Khandoba Secure Docs** icon on your home screen
2. The app will launch and show the welcome screen

---

## First Launch Setup

### Step 1: Sign In with Apple
1. Tap **"Sign In with Apple"**
2. Choose whether to share your email (optional)
3. Authenticate with Face ID or Touch ID
4. Grant permissions when prompted

### Step 2: Biometric Setup
1. The app will prompt you to enable Face ID or Touch ID
2. Follow on-screen instructions
3. This secures your vault access

### Step 3: Create Profile
1. Take a selfie for your profile (optional but recommended)
2. Enter your name (pre-filled from Apple Sign In)
3. Tap **"Continue"**

### Step 4: Subscription
1. Choose subscription plan:
   - **Monthly**: $9.99/month
   - **Yearly**: $71.88/year (Save 40%)
2. Start **7-day free trial**
3. Confirm purchase

### Step 5: Create First Vault
1. Tap **"Create Vault"**
2. Enter vault name (e.g., "Personal Documents")
3. Choose vault type:
   - **Single-Key**: Password protected
   - **Dual-Key**: Requires ML approval
4. Set password (if single-key)
5. Tap **"Create"**

## Permissions

The app will request the following permissions:

### Required Permissions
- **Camera**: For document scanning and selfie capture
- **Photo Library**: For uploading documents
- **Microphone**: For voice memos and recordings
- **Location**: For geographic security monitoring
- **Calendar**: For security review scheduling (optional)

### How to Grant Permissions
1. When prompted, tap **"Allow"**
2. Or go to **Settings → Khandoba Secure Docs** to manage permissions later

---

## Cloud Sync Setup

### Automatic Setup
- CloudKit sync is **automatic** after sign-in
- Your data syncs across all your devices
- No additional configuration needed

### Manual Sync (if needed)
1. Go to **Settings → iCloud**
2. Ensure **iCloud Drive** is enabled
3. Ensure **Khandoba Secure Docs** is enabled in iCloud

## Troubleshooting Installation

### App Won't Download
- **Check Storage**: Ensure you have enough free space
- **Check Internet**: Ensure you're connected to Wi-Fi or cellular
- **Restart**: Restart your device and try again
- **Update iOS**: Ensure you're running iOS 17.0 or later

### Sign In Issues
- **Check Apple ID**: Ensure you're signed in to iCloud
- **Check Internet**: Ensure you have internet connection
- **Try Again**: Close and reopen the app

### Permission Issues
- **Settings**: Go to Settings → Khandoba Secure Docs
- **Enable Permissions**: Toggle on required permissions
- **Restart App**: Close and reopen the app
