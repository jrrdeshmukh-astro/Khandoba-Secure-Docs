# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 18, 2025**

## Introduction

Khandoba Secure Docs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and services.

By using Khandoba Secure Docs, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: When you sign in with Apple, we collect your name and email address (if you choose to share it)
- **Profile Information**: Selfie photo for biometric verification
- **Documents**: Files, photos, videos, voice memos, and other content you upload to your vaults
- **Vault Information**: Vault names, descriptions, and security settings

### Information Automatically Collected

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, access patterns, session information
- **Location Data**: Geographic coordinates when accessing vaults (for security monitoring)
- **Technical Data**: IP addresses, timestamps, error logs

### Information from Third Parties

- **Apple Sign In**: Authentication credentials and user profile information
- **CloudKit**: Data synchronization and backup information
- **Supabase**: Cloud storage and database information (if enabled)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Core Functionality
- **Authentication**: Verify your identity using Apple Sign In and biometric authentication
- **Data Storage**: Securely store and encrypt your documents in vaults
- **Synchronization**: Sync your data across devices using CloudKit or Supabase
- **Access Control**: Manage vault access, dual-key approvals, and nominee permissions

### Security & Monitoring
- **Threat Detection**: Analyze access patterns, geographic anomalies, and suspicious activity using machine learning
- **Security Logging**: Track vault access, document operations, and security events
- **Fraud Prevention**: Detect impossible travel, unauthorized access attempts, and security breaches

### AI & Intelligence Features
- **Document Analysis**: Classify documents as source/sink, extract text, and generate AI tags
- **Voice Reports**: Generate narrated security briefings using text-to-speech
- **Intel Reports**: Cross-document analysis and narrative generation
- **ML Auto-Approval**: Automatically approve or deny vault access requests based on threat metrics

### Service Improvement
- **Analytics**: Understand app usage patterns (anonymized and aggregated)
- **Error Tracking**: Identify and fix bugs and performance issues
- **Feature Development**: Improve existing features and develop new ones

## Data Storage & Encryption

### Encryption Standards
- **AES-256 Encryption**: All documents are encrypted using military-grade AES-256 encryption
- **Zero-Knowledge Architecture**: We cannot decrypt your data - only you have the encryption keys
- **End-to-End Encryption**: Data is encrypted on your device before transmission

### Storage Locations
- **Local Storage**: Encrypted data stored on your device
- **CloudKit**: Apple's secure cloud storage (if enabled)
- **Supabase**: Secure cloud database and storage (if enabled)
- **No Third-Party Storage**: We do not use third-party cloud storage services

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Vault Deletion**: Deleted vaults and documents are permanently removed from all systems

## Information Sharing & Disclosure

### We Do NOT Share Your Data
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
- **No Advertising**: We do not use your data for advertising purposes
- **No Data Brokers**: We do not provide data to data brokers or analytics companies

### Limited Sharing Scenarios
- **Nominees**: You may share vault access with nominated users (with your explicit consent)
- **CloudKit/Supabase**: Data is stored in secure cloud services (encrypted and zero-knowledge)
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

### Service Providers
- **Apple**: Authentication and CloudKit services (subject to Apple's privacy policy)
- **Supabase**: Cloud database and storage (if enabled, subject to Supabase's privacy policy)
- **No Other Providers**: We do not use additional third-party service providers

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access & Control
- **View Your Data**: Access all your documents, vaults, and account information through the app
- **Export Data**: Download your documents and data at any time
- **Delete Data**: Delete individual documents, vaults, or your entire account

### Account Management
- **Update Information**: Modify your profile information and preferences
- **Revoke Access**: Remove nominees and revoke vault access at any time
- **Account Deletion**: Request complete account deletion (data permanently removed within 30 days)

### Biometric Data
- **Local Storage Only**: Biometric data (Face ID/Touch ID) is stored only on your device
- **No Cloud Storage**: Biometric templates are never transmitted or stored in the cloud
- **Device-Only**: Biometric authentication is processed entirely on your device

## Security Measures

### Technical Safeguards
- **AES-256 Encryption**: Military-grade encryption for all stored data
- **Zero-Knowledge Architecture**: We cannot access your decrypted data
- **Secure Transmission**: All data transmission uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Regular Security Audits**: Ongoing security assessments and updates

### Access Controls
- **Biometric Authentication**: Face ID/Touch ID for vault access
- **Dual-Key Vaults**: Two-person approval for sensitive vaults
- **Session Management**: Automatic vault locking after inactivity
- **Access Logging**: Complete audit trail of all vault and document access

### Threat Monitoring
- **ML-Based Detection**: Machine learning algorithms detect suspicious patterns
- **Geographic Anomaly Detection**: Identifies impossible travel and unauthorized access
- **Real-Time Alerts**: Immediate notifications for security events

## Children's Privacy

Khandoba Secure Docs is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Data Transfers

- **Data Location**: Your data may be stored in data centers located in the United States or other countries
- **Apple CloudKit**: Subject to Apple's data processing terms
- **Supabase**: Subject to Supabase's data processing terms
- **Adequate Safeguards**: All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws
