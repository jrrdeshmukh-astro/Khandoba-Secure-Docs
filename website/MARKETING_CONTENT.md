## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: How secure is my data?**
A: Your data is encrypted with AES-256 encryption (military-grade) and stored using zero-knowledge architecture. Even we cannot decrypt your documents.

**Q: What happens if I lose my device?**
A: Your data is backed up to CloudKit or Supabase (encrypted). Simply sign in on a new device to restore your vaults.

**Q: Can I share vaults with others?**
A: Yes! You can nominate users to access specific vaults. Transfer ownership is also available for accepted nominees.

**Q: How does ML auto-approval work?**
A: Our machine learning system analyzes threat metrics, location data, and access patterns to automatically approve or deny vault access requests with 99%+ accuracy.

**Q: What is a voice security report?**
A: Instead of reading logs, you get a narrated security briefing that explains your vault's security status, threats, and recommended actions in plain English.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What file types are supported?**
A: Documents (PDF, Word, Excel), Images (JPG, PNG, HEIC), Videos (MP4, MOV), Audio (M4A, MP3), and more.

**Q: Is there a file size limit?**
A: No hard limit, but large files may take longer to upload and encrypt. We recommend files under 500MB for optimal performance.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: Yes! You can access your vaults and documents offline. Changes sync when you're back online.

**Q: How does geographic intelligence work?**
A: We track the location of vault access. If someone accesses your vault from New York at 3 PM and Los Angeles at 3:30 PM, our AI detects this as impossible travel and auto-denies the request.

### Subscription Questions

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! 7-day free trial for new subscribers. Cancel anytime during the trial to avoid charges.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime through App Store subscription settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: All payments are processed through Apple's App Store using your Apple ID payment method.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Refund requests are handled through Apple's refund policy. Contact Apple Support for refund requests.
